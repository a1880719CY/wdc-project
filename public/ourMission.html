What's our mission:

At the International Division of Kindness (IDK),

our aim is to raise awareness about the importance of

protecting wildlife. We promote the correct and humane ways

to interact with animals, fostering respect and compassion for all

living beings. Through education and community engagement,

we strive to create a world where humans and animals

coexist harmoniously.
